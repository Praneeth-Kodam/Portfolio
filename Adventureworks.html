Adventureworks

Description

As part of my portfolio, I developed a comprehensive Power BI dashboard for AdventureWorks, a fictional retail company. The goal of this project was to provide actionable insights into key performance indicators across multiple business dimensions, including sales, product performance, profitability, and regional trends.

Below is a breakdown of the dashboard:

1. Overview Page

This page presents a high-level view of key metrics, including:

  1. Total Sales: A bar chart visualizes sales performance across different time periods.
  2. Profit Margin by Month: A line chart illustrates the monthly profit margins, helping to track profitability trends over time.
  3. Total Sales by Region and Category: A segmented bar chart shows how different regions and product categories contribute to the overall sales.
  4. Total Quantity by Product: A chart displaying total quantities sold per product, offering insight into product popularity.

2. Profits Page

To analyze profitability in greater detail, I incorporated:

  1. Scatterplot: Visualizing the relationship between profit and profit margin against total sales.
  2. Matrix: Displays key metrics (sales, profit, and profit margin) broken down by category and subcategory, providing a detailed breakdown of performance.
  3. Line Chart: This shows Sales Year-to-Date (YTD) and Year-over-Year (YoY) growth, giving a clear view of sales trends by product.

3. Product Analysis Page

I used parameterized charts to enable dynamic analysis based on user input. The two parameters are:

  1. Dimension Parameter: Includes Category, Subcategory, and Product.
  2. Measure Parameter: Includes Sales, Quantity, Profit, and Profit Margin. These parameters allow for customized views in both a bar chart (Sales by selected parameters) and a matrix chart, enabling flexible, user-driven analysis.

4. Trends Page

The focus here is on understanding overall sales and order trends:

  1. Total Sales by Month: A column chart displaying monthly sales performance.
  2. Profit by Month: A waterfall chart showing how profit changes over time.
  3. Orders by Month: A column chart tracking the number of orders month by month.
  4. Top 10 Products: A bar chart ranking the highest-selling products.

5. Regions Page

This page helps analyze geographical sales performance:

  1. Total Sales by Region (Map): map chart showing sales distribution across different regions.
  2. Total Sales by Region Group: A bar chart comparing sales across regional groups, providing insights into regional market strengths.
